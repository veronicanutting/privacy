Website Privacy Policy Statement
When you visit harvard.com, we collect aggregate information on what pages you visit. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our webpage, and is not shared with other organizations for any purpose. Your personal information is not tied to the data.
We use cookies to record items that you add to your shopping cart.
We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse, or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.
